FAQS

Why does STARPATH ask for my state location and applicable grade level(s)?
STARPATH does not track any sort of location and simply asks for a state location to make posted resources more specific to each community. Exact locations are not required and are not suggested to share online but state locations allow for people from each state to share sources from their state with others from the same state and across the nation. These resources will also be tagged per state (like hashtags) and will even show up on our state map on our homepage!

Grade levels will be used like a hashtag to help narrow resources to students per grade level in high school and after graduation. This will help source finding to be more efficient especially for high school juniors and seniors looking for scholarships, volunteer opportunities, and more.
How do the hashtags work?
Hashtags allow you to explore and post pages under a similar topic. If a hashtag isn’t already made as a preset, don’t hesitate to post it under a new hashtag! It’ll work just the same.
What should I caption my post?
Captions/additional information is not required but is highly recommended as it’ll boost the outreach of the post. We suggest that your caption contains information related to what you are posting.
How do upvotes work?
Each post has an upvote button, more upvotes means that more people have found this post helpful, meaning that it is more reliable in the community! If any inappropriate content is found, don’t hesitate to report it by contacting us.
My resource isn’t publishing correctly, what should I do?
If a post is made incorrectly, reload the page and check again. If it won’t allow you to post, check if you’ve filled out required boxes and are not posting inappropriate content.
